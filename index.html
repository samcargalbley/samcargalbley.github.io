About

This began as an attempt to bring some order to the places, ideas, and people I find myself returning to. My interests don’t follow a single line — film, soccer (largely to keep pace with my American friends), music, and a still-developing curiosity about art all sit alongside a growing fascination with the energy transition and business development.

For now, the latter occupies most of my attention. While you wait — perhaps patiently, perhaps not — for thoughts on the former, I hope you’ll spend some time exploring it here.

Eventually, when confidence catches up with habit, I’d like this to become a space for my own writing: a place to collect observations, unfinished ideas, and questions about markets, infrastructure, and the systems that shape everyday life mostly unnoticed. Think of it less as a finished argument and more as a record of things I’m trying to understand.
