About

Work in progress.

This began as an attempt to bring some order to the places, ideas, and people I find myself returning to. My interests don’t follow a single line — film, soccer, music, and an underdeveloped curiosity about art all sit alongside my fascination with the energy transition and the forces that drive how business grow and compete.

So while you wait, perhaps impatiently, for thoughts on the former, I hope you’ll take some time to explore the latter.

Eventually, when confidence catches up with intention, I’d like this to become a space for my own writing: a place for observations, unfinished ideas, and questions (and attempts at answers) about the energy transition, infrastructure, and the systems that shape everyday life mostly unnoticed. Think of it less as a finished argument and more as a record of things I’m trying to understand.
